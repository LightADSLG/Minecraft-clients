Clients & Hacked Clients

What are Clients?

In Minecraft, a client is a version or variation of the game's launcher and interface. There are many clients with additional features for better FPS, visual adjustments, and quality-of-life improvements.

What are Hacked Clients?

Hacked clients are modified versions of the game that provide unfair advantages, like speed, auto-aim, and more. While these can be used on certain servers, using hacked clients is often against server rules and may lead to bans.

Minecraft Bedrock Clients

Unlike Java Edition, Minecraft Bedrock Edition has its own set of clients designed to enhance gameplay. These clients offer optimizations, unique features, and smoother performance tailored to Bedrock's codebase.

  • Onix Client
  • Horion Client
